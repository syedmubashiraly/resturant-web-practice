Chowmein





















At Fusion Bazaar Eatery, our Chow Mein is a delightful fusion of Chinese and Middle Eastern flavors, offering a unique twist on this beloved dish. We start by stir-frying a medley of fresh vegetables such as bell peppers, carrots, onions, and cabbage in a wok, infusing them with aromatic spices and seasonings. These vibrant vegetables add color, texture, and nutrition to our Chow Mein. Next, we add your choice of protein, whether it's succulent chicken, tender beef, or juicy shrimp. The protein is quickly cooked to perfection, locking in its natural juices and flavors.
