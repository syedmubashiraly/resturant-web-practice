Chinese Rice













At Fusion Bazaar Eatery, our Fried Rice is a delicious fusion of flavors inspired by both Chinese and Middle Eastern cuisine, creating a unique and satisfying dish that's sure to please your palate. We start by stir-frying cooked rice in a wok with a blend of aromatic spices, including garlic, ginger, and green onions, which infuse the rice with rich flavor and aroma. This creates a flavorful base for our Fried Rice. Next, we add a colorful array of fresh vegetables, such as carrots, peas, bell peppers, and onions, which add texture, nutrition, and visual appeal to the dish. These vegetables are quickly cooked until they're tender yet still crisp, retaining their natural sweetness and vibrant colors. For protein, you have the option to add your choice of tender chicken, juicy shrimp, or savory beef, which are cooked to perfection and then added to the rice and vegetable mixture.
