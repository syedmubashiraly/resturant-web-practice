Kababs





















At Fusion Bazaar Eatery, our Pakistani kababs are a celebration of flavor and tradition. We craft each kabab with the finest quality meats, expertly marinated and grilled to perfection. From the classic Seekh Kababs made with spiced minced meat to the succulent Boti Kababs featuring tender chunks of marinated meat, our menu offers a tantalizing selection of kababs that will satisfy even the most discerning palate. What sets our Pakistani kababs apart is the meticulous attention to detail in our marinades and the perfect balance of spices that infuse each bite with rich, complex flavors. Whether you prefer your kababs spicy and fiery or mild and aromatic, we have something for everyone.
