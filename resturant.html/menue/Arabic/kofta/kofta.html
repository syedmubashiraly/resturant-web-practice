Kofta





















At Fusion Bazaar Eatery, our Arabic kofta is a tantalizing blend of flavors that captures the essence of Middle Eastern cuisine. Made with premium quality ground meat, aromatic spices, and fresh herbs, our kofta is a culinary delight that will transport you to the vibrant markets of the Arabian Peninsula. We start by meticulously blending the finest cuts of meat, whether it's lamb, beef, or a combination of both, with a medley of traditional spices such as cumin, coriander, paprika, and cinnamon. Freshly chopped parsley, cilantro, and mint add a burst of freshness and aroma to the mixture.
