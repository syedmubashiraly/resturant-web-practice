Shawarma





















At Fusion Bazaar Eatery, our Shawarma is a mouthwatering blend of tender meat, aromatic spices, and flavorful condiments that will transport you to the bustling streets of the Middle East with every bite. The marinated meat is then slowly roasted on a vertical rotisserie, allowing it to cook evenly and develop a crispy, caramelized exterior while remaining juicy and tender on the inside. Once cooked to perfection, the meat is thinly sliced and piled high onto warm, fluffy pita bread or freshly baked flatbread. It's then topped with a variety of delicious condiments, such as tangy tahini sauce, creamy garlic sauce, crunchy pickles, and crisp lettuce, tomato, and onion.
